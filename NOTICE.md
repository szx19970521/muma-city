Muma City / 牧马城市
====================

Muma City is a derivative work based on Hermes Desktop.

Upstream project:
- Hermes Desktop
- https://github.com/fathah/hermes-desktop
- MIT License

The original MIT license text and copyright notice are preserved in
LICENSE. Modifications in this repository add the Muma City / 牧马城市
game-like 3D office experience, Office3D scene code, character/avatar
experiments, first-person interaction experiments, and related UI changes.

Third-party 3D and animation assets used by Office3D are documented in:
- src/renderer/src/screens/Office/office3d/assets/THIRD_PARTY_ATTRIBUTION.json
- src/renderer/src/screens/Office/office3d/assets/FIRST_PERSON_ATTRIBUTION.json

Additional asset references found in experimental Office3D content:
- Wooden Table, Sketchfab model: https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/wooden-table-0dc1c7d6cbab4d74bef7c4f82abf2caf
- Sofa Chair, Sketchfab model: https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/sofa-chair-0e8e009f398249b9bc13e8ff7078530a

Before making a production release, review the attribution files and remove
any experimental assets that are not intended to ship.
